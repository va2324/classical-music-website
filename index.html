Classical Music

Classical music consists of a broad range of music spanning a vast time period, from the end of the Renaissance period to the beginning of the 20th century. It is played with percussion instruments, string instruments, woodwind instruments, and brass instruments, such as the ones shown below.

Contrary to popular belief, classical music is not one uniform style of music. Rather, classical music consists of a diverse number of styles that varied throughout the centuries. Classical music can be divided into three distinct eras: the Baroque Era, the Classical Era, and the Romantic Era.
